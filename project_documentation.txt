CAMPUS MARKET PROJECT DOCUMENTATION
==================================

OVERVIEW
--------
Campus Market is a specialized e-commerce platform designed for university students, 
enabling them to buy, sell, and trade within their campus community. The project 
emphasizes trust, ease of use, and professional features for student entrepreneurs.

USER ROLES & AUTHENTICATION
---------------------------
- Regular Member (Buyer/Student): Can browse, buy, message sellers, and save listings.
- Pro Member (Seller/Vendor): Verified students who can list products, access sales 
  analytics, and manage their business via a professional dashboard.
- Verification: Student verification is a core trust feature, often displayed in 
  the profile details.

CORE SECTIONS & FEATURES
------------------------

1. HOME & LANDING PAGE
   - Feature: Hero section with campus-specific branding.
   - Content: Featured categories, trending products, and quick-links to popular items.
   - Usage: Discovering the latest campus deals and navigating to specific niches.

2. SEARCH & LISTINGS
   - Feature: Advanced filtering by category, price, and condition.
   - Content: Product cards showing prices, high-quality images, and seller ratings.
   - Usage: Finding specific textbooks, electronics, or dorm essentials quickly.

3. PRODUCT DETAIL PAGE
   - Feature: Real-time messaging, "Save for Later," and share options.
   - Content: Detailed descriptions, policies, and "More from this seller" sections.
   - Usage: Reviewing product specs before committing to a purchase or inquiry.

4. USER PROFILES
   - Regular Profile: A clean, student-focused view showing verified status and "About" info.
   - Professional Profile: A premium header for sellers featuring ratings, total sales, 
     active items, and business info like return policies and hours.
   - Usage: Building trust between buyers and sellers via membership history and reviews.

5. PROFESSIONAL DASHBOARD (Pro Only)
   - Overview: High-level sales revenue charts, total orders, and store health stats.
   - Products: Inventory management system to add, edit, or pause listings.
   - Orders: Fulfillment tracker for pending and completed transactions.
   - Usage: A centralized workspace for student businesses to scale and manage sales.

6. COMMUNICATION HUB
   - Messages: A unified workspace for buyer-seller negotiations and support.
   - Alerts: Real-time notifications for price drops, new listings, and system updates.
   - Usage: Staying connected and responsive within the campus marketplace.

7. HELP & SUPPORT
   - Help Center: FAQs, chat supportrooms, and resolution centers for disputes.
   - Usage: Resolving technical issues or transaction disagreements.

8. ADMIN PANEL
   - Seller Approvals: A tool for administrators to verify IDs and business applications.
   - Content Management: Moderation of listings and user reports.
   - Usage: Maintaining marketplace safety and verifying authentic student vendors.

9. SAVED ITEMS & SETTINGS
   - Saved: A dedicated space for users to track items they are interested in.
   - Account Settings: Management of personal info, notifications, and "Upgrade to Pro" flows.
   - Usage: Customizing the platform experience and managing user data.

TECHNICAL STACK
---------------
- Core: Next.js (App Router), Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion for animations.
- Icons: Lucide React.
- State: AuthContext for role-based access control.
